Maps

Each match of VALORANT takes place on a map. Maps feature a spawn area for each team, barriers that limit accessible areas during the Buy Phase, and objective sites where the spike is to be planted. Four maps were available at launch, and a six-month cycle is expected for the release of new maps. There are currently eight playable maps in VALORANT, with an additional one for practice and training new players.
